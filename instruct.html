Instructions for Uploading Fingerprints

Step 1: Choose How to Capture Your Fingerprints
1. Using a Mobile App (Recommended – Most Accurate): Use a dedicated fingerprint SDK app such as iSL SDK App for mobile devices. This method captures high-quality prints suitable for our analysis.
   Steps:
    a. Install the iSL SDK App from the Play Store.
    b. Register yourself within the app.
    c. Navigate to the Fingerprint Capture section.
    d. Place your finger on the sensor and capture the fingerprint.
    e. Take a screenshot of the captured fingerprint image.
    f. Upload this screenshot in the corresponding section on the dashboard (ensure the correct finger section is selected).

2. Using Ink Pad and Paper: Press your finger on an ink pad and then roll it onto a clean white paper to get a clear print.

Step 2: Take Clear Pictures
Make sure the image is sharp and in focus. Ensure good lighting—avoid shadows or bright glare. Capture the whole finger in the picture; it should clearly show the pattern of your fingerprint.

Step 3: Uploading
Go to the “Upload Fingerprints” section on the dashboard.
Select your files for each finger. (Upload the correct fingerprint in every section for accurate results, e.g., upload Right Thumb in Right Thumb section only.)

Tips:
- Make sure the finger is centered in the photo.
- Avoid blurred, smudged, or incomplete prints.
- For additional guidance, watch: Fingerprint Capture Tutorial


⚠️ Note: Not following the above instructions may lead to wrong or inaccurate results.
